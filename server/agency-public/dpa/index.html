This Data Processing Agreement ("DPA") forms part of the Terms of Service or Master Service Agreement between Ainexlify Agencies ("Data Processor" / "Service Provider") and the Client ("Data Controller" / "Business").

1. Definitions and Relationship of the Parties

In the context of the services provided by Ainexlify Agencies to the Client:

▪ The Client

Acts strictly as the "Data Controller" (under PIPEDA and general data protection principles) or "Business" (under CCPA). The Client determines the purposes and means of processing personal data.

▪ Ainexlify Agencies

Acts strictly as the "Data Processor" or "Service Provider". We process personal data solely on behalf of the Client and in accordance with the Client’s documented instructions.

2. Processing of Personal Data

Ainexlify Agencies will only process personal data to provide the AI lead generation services outlined in the principal agreement. We will not retain, use, or disclose the personal data for any purpose other than for the specific purpose of performing the services, or as otherwise permitted by the CCPA and PIPEDA.

Client Responsibility

The Client expressly acknowledges and agrees that they are solely responsible for ensuring that all SMS, text messages, phone calls, and other communications handled by Layla or any Ainexlify Agencies service strictly comply with all applicable telemarketing and anti-spam laws.

Compliance

This includes, but is not limited to, obtaining all necessary prior express written consents required under the United States Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA) and the Canadian Anti-Spam Legislation (CASL).

Indemnification

The Client agrees to indemnify and hold harmless Ainexlify Agencies against any claims, fines, or damages arising from the Client's failure to comply with the TCPA, CASL, or any other applicable communication laws.

Ainexlify Agencies implements appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure a level of security appropriate to the risk. Upon termination of the services or upon the Client's written request, Ainexlify Agencies will securely delete or return all personal data to the Client, unless further storage is required by applicable law.
